Romanticizing the Gods

Buy This Book On Amazon

Gods, monsters and creatures have existed before the dawn of time.

In this anthology you’ll receive a variation of different twists on mythological gods, monsters and creatures.

Norse are known for their brutality, as well as their sacrifices. Greek and Romans show their love of the arts, wine and music, though will fight for anything they wish to conquer. Egyptians show their rise to power and their relentless determination to keep on the same path, tearing down anyone in their way. Meanwhile, Celtics are the first faeries to wander this realm.

Suspense, romance and intrigue. What could happen to your favorite gods, demi-gods, monsters and more? Dive into Romanticizing the Gods to find out.

I happen to be a Southern born and raised author who took the long way around to get there. Born in Texas, raised in the cloud of the oilfield, and then moved to Tennessee to pursue success in textiles and hosiery mills. This rearing in heavy industry, the sort that turns boys into men, shaped my perspectives on what is work, what is play, and oddly enough, what makes a good story.

A lifelong reader, long aspiring writer, a pen and paper gamer, an amateur baker, horse owner, and enthusiast of things that are weird and wonderful, I take these things and use them to craft weird tales, dark romance, and whatever else crossed my improvised desk and workspace.

I live in an insular small East TN town with my wife of 18 years.
